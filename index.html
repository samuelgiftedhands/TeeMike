About Us

TeeMike is a duly registered Building and Construction Company based in Lagos, Nigeria. We provide exceptional and professional services in the area of architecture, landscaping, civil engineering and furniture supplies.

With over 10years of professional productive journey, the company is relentlessly making effort to standout when it comes to meeting and satisfying human needs in the area of shelter.

Our core strength is in the combination of human and advanced technological resources put into planning, designing, down to the final stage of construction which has enabled us to maintain a leading role in Building and Construction business in Nigeria.

We pride ourselves in rendering quality services while engaging with the best form of technology to scale our building processes. The scale of our projects ranges from individual homes to estate services. We are also dedicated to building homes for individuals across the African states. Our clients can attest to the strong interpersonal relationship approach we take to bringing them satisfaction. We go on to earn their confidence and trust as always in delivering a prompt A+ grade job at all times.
